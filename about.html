I am a data analyst who enjoys solving problems with data-driven solutions. I feel that data holds valuable information that can be unlocked and harnessed to drive quantifiable improvement. I use SQL, Python, and business intelligence tools to achieve this.

I am educated in data extraction, data cleaning, data transformation, and dashboard development. I have used these skills to drive improvements throughout the Idaho court system and in the private sector.

My data analysis services have been used in the state of Idaho to improve best practices for sentencing terms, assist in the evaluation of domestic violence courts, monitor the treatment of minors in our court system, and help ensure equal access to justice. Furthermore, my skills have been used by executives, managers, and engineers to drive process improvements and monitor costs in the construction industry.
